Hi, I'm Eric Ross Bernstein

I'm a web developer, designer and artist based in California.

About Me

I'm a full-stack developer proficient in Javascript, React, Node.js, HTML and CSS. I also have 10 years of experience as a freelance designer and architect for clients including Disney, Target, Honda, Nintendo, Progressive, AT&T and Dell. My expertise in 2D/3D design programs and my years of experience in remote work have instilled me with creative problem solving skills, self-motivation, and a high attention to detail.
