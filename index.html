Hi, I'm Eric Ross Bernstein

I'm an artist, designer, and web developer based in California.

About Me

After graduating with a degree in architecture from Cornell University, I co-founded the design collective Hither Yon in 2011. From 2012 to 2014, I worked as an architect at Studio Jeppe Hein in Berlin, designing various public art projects throughout Europe and the United States. I have exhibited my artwork in Berlin, New York, LA, Rome, Turin, Milan, and Copenhagen, and my illustrations have been featured on Booooooom and Supersonic Art.

I'm currently studying software engineering at Thinkful, where I've discovered a particular passion for JavaScript functions and clean, simple web design. If you're looking for a web developer with an eye for detail and a willingness to learn, I would love to collaborate with you. I'm always in search of challenging projects that combine my interests in art, design, and web development.
